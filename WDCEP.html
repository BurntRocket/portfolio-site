WDCEP

Site Architecture, Ux & UI design

The Washington, DC Economic Partnership provides information, events, and recourses for development and business opportunities in the District of Columbia. Their mission is to support the economic well being of our city by encouraging existing and new businesses to open up shop in the District. With this in mind, we are currently helping them re-target their website. Currently, it is about an organization that does really great work. Instead, we are directing the message towards why DC is special and how you, the user, can get information about it.
